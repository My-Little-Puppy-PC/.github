## About My Little Puppy

My Little Puppy is a casual pet simulation game where players interact with and take care of a virtual dog. The experience focuses on feeding, training, playing, and building a connection with your puppy over time in a calm and friendly environment.

Players can customize their puppy, adjust its surroundings, and participate in small activities that help progression and development. The game is designed around simple mechanics, making it accessible while still offering interactive engagement through care systems and light progression features.

With a cozy atmosphere, easy controls, and a focus on interaction and routine, My Little Puppy delivers a relaxing gameplay experience built around companionship, customization, and gradual development.
